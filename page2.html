My Ideal Vacation Spots

I want to see Maine with its New England styles, beautiful salted lighthouses, and Acadia National Park - every photo of it reminds me of an Autumn paradise. I want to dip my feet into its tidepools, hike among its firs and pines, and take in the crisp salty air of its promontories.

Crater Lake is a deep volcanic well with crystal clear waters and an island of wizards in its center. I want to feel the resounding heartbeat of the caldera as I peer into its endless depths.

Playing the video game Fallout: New Vegas I've always wanted to venture to this place to see all the locations I visited in my playthrough. Not only do the photos give a sense of grandeur but there is a deep feeling of nostalgia that comes with each capture of the canyons, rivers, and orange shrub-filled paths.
